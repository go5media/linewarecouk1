About Us

What is Line Ware?
Line Ware exists because great tradespeople deserve to look like great businesses. A skilled carpenter, plumber or electrician shouldn't have to spend evenings trying to figure out email settings or phone systems. That's our job.

We set up and manage your website, professional email and phone system so you can focus on the work you're actually good at. Everything is handled by us, you just use it.

Why we built it this way?
Most digital services hand you a set of tools and leave you to figure it out. We don't. We set everything up, we manage it, and when something needs changing you call us. One person, one number, everything sorted.

Who we work with?
Small trade businesses across the UK who are brilliant at what they do but need a professional digital presence to match.
