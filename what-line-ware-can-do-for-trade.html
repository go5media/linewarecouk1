How Do I Keep My Clients Coming Back To My Plumbing Business Using Technology?

Line Ware - What Can You Do For My Trade Business

What Is A Managed Service Provider?

Line Ware is a Managed Service Provider, or MSP. A Managed Service Provider is a company that sets up, runs, and maintains technology on your behalf, so you never have to touch it yourself. Rather than handing you a set of tools and leaving you to figure it out, an MSP takes full responsibility for making sure everything works, stays up to date, and gets fixed when something goes wrong. You get the result without doing the technical work.
For a trade business, that means your website, your professional email address, and your business phone number are all handled by one person, on one monthly bill, with one number to call if you ever need anything changed or fixed. No logging into dashboards. No calling three different companies. No spending your evenings trying to work out why your email has stopped working. You just use it.

How Does That Help Your Business?

Every hour you spend troubleshooting technology is an hour you are not on the tools, not quoting a job, and not earning money. A managed service removes that friction entirely. Your digital presence, the website a potential client finds on Google, the email address your quote arrives from, the phone number on your van, all of it is set up properly, maintained consistently, and updated whenever you need it, without you being involved in any of it.
The result is a business that looks and operates professionally from day one, without requiring you to become a technical expert to get there.

How Our Service Works

Line Ware operates on a simple flat monthly fee. You choose the plan that suits your business, pay one amount each month, and everything covered under that plan is handled for you, no surprise invoices, no hidden costs, and no additional charges for the things your plan includes.

Your website updates, your email configuration, and your call costs are all managed within your monthly fee. If your business number needs to route calls to your mobile, that is covered. If you are on a higher tier and making outbound calls from your business number, those call costs are included too. You do not need to think about what each individual thing costs because it is all wrapped into one predictable monthly payment.

As your business grows or your needs change, you can add to your plan with optional addons that sit on top of your base package. You only pay for what you actually need, and you can adjust as your business develops.

This is what being a Managed Service Provider means in practice,one relationship, one bill, and one number to call when you need anything. Everything else is our problem, not yours.

LW Pro

LW Pro is the foundation of every Line Ware service. Included in the base plan is a professionally built and managed website, a business email address on your own domain that forwards directly to your existing inbox, and a dedicated business phone number that diverts inbound calls straight to your mobile. Even on the base plan your phone number comes with a professional greeting that reassures the caller they have reached the right company before the call connects. Automatic call recording is included as standard, as soon as a call ends, a recording is sent directly to your email so you can listen back at any time. If you cannot answer, voicemail to email ensures the caller can leave a message which is then sent straight to your inbox as an audio file. You also get a whisper message when you pick up, which plays a short prompt asking you to press 1 to accept the call, so you always know whether an incoming call is a business enquiry or a personal call before you answer. Flexible forwarding means you can divert to multiple numbers, choosing whether calls ring simultaneously or one after another. For example, if two business owners are both on site, calls can ring both at once so whoever is free picks up first. Alternatively you can set calls to ring the office contact first and divert to the person on site if there is no answer, with voicemail as the final fallback if nobody is available.

For your business, LW Pro means that the next time a potential client searches for a carpenter, plumber, or electrician in your area, they find a professionally built website rather than a Facebook page or nothing at all. They can see your services, your operating areas, and examples of your previous work. When they are ready to get in touch they have three ways to do it, a phone number they can call, a business email address they can write to, and an enquiry form they can fill in directly from the site. Having your own domain email address also means your clients see a professional address on your van, your invoices, and your quotes rather than a generic free address, which matters more than most people realise before they have one. Your website also works alongside your Google Business Profile rather than in competition with it. Linking the two means potential clients can see your reviews and your listed services on Google, and then click through to a full professional website that presents your work exactly the way you want it presented. No website means you are only as visible as your Google profile allows. With one, you have two separate surfaces working together to bring in enquiries.

LW Plus Addon

LW Plus transforms your phone system from an inbound service into a fully operational business communications platform. The centrepiece of the addon is the 3CX mobile app, which allows you to make outbound calls from your business number directly from your phone, so when you call a client back, they see your business number on their screen rather than your personal mobile. The same app handles your inbound calls, your voicemail, and your call recordings all in one place. Rather than receiving recordings by email as you do on LW Pro, with LW Plus you can open the recordings tab in the app and listen back to any call directly. Voicemail works the same way, messages appear in the app and can be played back instantly without needing to check your email. You also gain full control over when you are available to take calls. By logging in and out of ring groups you can stop business calls reaching you outside of working hours while still remaining reachable on your direct extension for existing clients or emergency contacts. For those moments when you want complete silence, Do Not Disturb turns off every incoming call from every source with a single tap.

LW Plus also adds a live chat widget to your website, connected directly to your mobile app. A visitor on your site can send a message which arrives on your phone instantly, and if the conversation develops to a point where a call makes more sense, the client can escalate from chat to a voice call through the website without either of you needing to dial a number. This is handled through a click to call button on your site which connects the caller directly through their browser, no phone number to dial, no app to download, just a button that connects them to you. For clients browsing on a desktop or laptop this removes the friction of having to pick up a separate device to make a call.

Your call queue experience also improves significantly with LW Plus. Rather than hearing a dial tone while waiting to be connected as they would on the base plan, your callers hear hold music or a custom message while they wait. That message is an opportunity, a short recording about your business, how long you have been operating, the kinds of jobs you take on, or a current promotion can play while the caller waits. A caller who has spent thirty seconds hearing about your twenty years of experience and your current ten percent discount for new customers is already warmer by the time you pick up than one who has been listening to a dial tone. We recommend including an offer in your hold message specifically because it gives the caller a reason to stay on the line and a reason to mention it when you answer.

LW One Addon

LW One is the addon that completes your email setup. While LW Pro gives you a professional business email address that forwards incoming messages to your existing inbox, LW One takes this further by giving you a full email client, meaning you can both receive and send emails directly from your business address without any complicated configuration. Your clients receive emails from your domain address and your replies come from the same place. Everything looks and works exactly as a professional business email should.

Powered by Microsoft 365, LW One also gives you 1TB of cloud storage per user, which you can use to store job photos, documents, quotes, and project files. Sharing with clients is straightforward you can share a folder or a file directly from your storage and your client can see exactly how their project is progressing at the tap of a button, without you needing to use WhatsApp or email attachments to send files back and forth.

Microsoft 365 also gives you access to the full suite of Microsoft applications including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook across web, mobile, and desktop. These are the full desktop versions of the software, not browser-only alternatives, which means you have professional grade tools available on any device you work from.

If you prefer the Google interface and are already comfortable working within Google's ecosystem, we can set you up on Google Workspace instead at no extra cost. The experience is similar and the email setup works the same way. The key differences are that Google Workspace provides web and mobile versions of its applications only, there are no desktop software licences, and you receive 2TB of storage rather than 1TB. Both options give you a fully functioning professional business email. It simply comes down to which platform suits the way you already work.

Cancellation, Contracts, and What Happens If Something Goes Wrong

Line Ware operates on a contract basis, with standard terms of 12 or 24 months depending on what suits your business. Contract length can be customised to your particular situation, though pricing may vary depending on the terms agreed. Your contract details, including the exact length and monthly cost, will be clearly set out before you sign anything.

During your first two months, which includes the £100 introductory period if you take that offer, there is no cancellation fee. You can cancel at any point during months one and two with just 7 days notice and you will owe nothing beyond what you have already paid. We want you to feel confident that you are trying the service without any financial risk.

From month three and four, a cancellation fee of £75 applies with 7 days notice. From month five onwards, the cancellation fee remains £75 but the notice period extends to 30 days. The cancellation fee exists to cover the real costs involved in winding down your service your domain name may be held for the remainder of its registration year, your phone number requires time to decommission, and there is time involved in the work already completed on your behalf. It is a cost recovery measure, not a deterrent.

If You Are Having Issues With Our Service

If you are experiencing problems with your service and that is the reason you want to cancel, we do not simply process the cancellation and move on. We will acknowledge the problem, offer you two free months of service, and commit to resolving the issue within that period. If at the end of those two months you are still not satisfied, we will waive the cancellation fee entirely. No argument, no negotiation.

The two free months begin from the point at which you are paying the full monthly rate, so if you are still within your introductory period when the issue arises, the clock starts when full billing begins.

If We Cancel Your Contract

If Line Ware terminates your contract for any reason other than non-payment, abuse, or fraud, you will receive your final month of service completely free of charge as an apology. Depending on the circumstances, we may also extend your web services for an additional month to give you time to find an alternative provider and ensure your business is not left without a digital presence overnight.

Any cancellation fee can be waived at our discretion where there is a valid reason to do so. We treat every situation individually and we will always try to do the right thing.
